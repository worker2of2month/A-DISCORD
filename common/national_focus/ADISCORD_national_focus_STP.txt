###
### Layout philosophy:
### - Central spine: Ivanov's final days and the collapse of the old peace.
### - West: Nodrul ideological and institutional leverage over the Party.
### - East: Kefreite mercenary pressure on highland resources.
### - Inner board: highlands, ports, palace, street, and garrisons.
### - Bridge outcomes are hidden and should be auto-completed by event.
###
### Layout note:
### - This version uses vertical layers to prevent sector collisions.
### - Foreign actors sit above the inner political board.
### - Ports, highlands, and palace occupy the middle layer.
### - Street and garrisons occupy the lower layer.
### - Bridge outcomes remain hidden and are meant to be completed by event.
